## What is Carbon?

[Carbon Design System](https://www.carbondesignsystem.com/), (or simply Carbon), is IBM’s open source design system for products and digital experiences. With the IBM Design Language as its foundation, the system consists of working code, design tools and resources, human interface guidelines, and a vibrant community of contributors.

## What is Carbon for IBM.com?

Carbon for IBM.com is built with Carbon at its foundation, with additional components and styling optimized for reading and the expressive nature of IBM.com's editorial experiences, and serves a wide range of designers and developers that are building digital products and experiences. Choosing Carbon as the strategic solution for Carbon for IBM.com ensures IBM has one consolidated design system across product and web. Carbon for IBM.com works alongside Carbon to produce components, patterns, and guidelines to leverage the IBM Design Language for IBM.com’s more expressive nature. It is maintained by the Digital Design Squad, a core team of designers, developers, and writers.

The goals of the Carbon for IBM.com include improving visual consistency and quality for all IBM.com website pages, establishing a shared user-centered experience for all IBM.com visitors, and providing clear, discoverable guidance around design and development best practices to all Carbon for IBM.com adopters.


## What are the key differences between Carbon and Carbon for IBM.com?

**Carbon for IBM.com is for IBM.com experiences.** 
The Carbon Design System is for product experiences.

**Carbon for IBM.com is built with [Web Components](https://ibmdotcom-web-components.mybluemix.net/?path=/story/overview-getting-started--page) and [React](https://ibmdotcom-react.mybluemix.net/?path=/story/overview-getting-started--page).**
The Carbon Design System is built with React first and supports core parts of the system in Vanilla JS, Angular, Vue, and Svelte.

**Carbon for IBM.com uses the [Expressive type sets](https://www.carbondesignsystem.com/guidelines/typography/expressive/).** The Carbon Design System uses the Productive type sets.

**Carbon for IBM.com is developed and maintained by the Digital Design Squad.** 
The Carbon Design System is developed and maintained by the Carbon Design System team.
