## Default

This component extends  of Carbon Design System's [default tabs](https://carbondesignsystem.com/components/tabs/usage/). On desktop, the component renders as tabs, allowing for large amount of content while keeping the page short. On mobile, it is renders as an accordion, keeping the longer tab labels easily readable.

The tab labels may wrap up to two lines then truncated.
