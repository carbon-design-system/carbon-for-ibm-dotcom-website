## Content guidance

| Field | Field type | Required   | Cardinality | Max character size (English / translated) | Notes | 
| -------- | --------- | ---------- | ---------- | ----------| ----------|
| Headline | Short copy | Yes | 1 | Mini (25 / 35)| - |
