## Default

Default Content block – segmented allows you to break up the narrative content into sections. It includes a heading, introductory paragraph, subsections, optional CTA (call-to-action), optional media (image or video), and horizontal rule.

![Image of content block simple default](../../images/pattern/content-block-segmented/content-block-segmented.jpg)

## Variations

### With aside elements
This variation includes navigational quick links on the right side. These quick links provide an alternate route directing users to resources related to the content in the main body.

![Image of content block simple with aside elements](../../images/pattern/content-block-segmented/content-block-segmented-with-aside-elements.jpg)
