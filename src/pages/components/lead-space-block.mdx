## Overview

When designed and implemented effectively, the Lead space block can be used to engage visitors immediately in one of the
most important moments in the user’s journey through a complex web environment.

It includes a headline, subheadline, supporting message,
[Link list](https://www.ibm.com/standards/carbon/react/?path=/story/components-linklist--default), and a call to action.
There is also the option to add media in the form of an image or video.
