## Default

The video component defaults to aspect ratio matching source video, or use one of the standard aspect ratios specified below. In most cases 16x9 is recommended.

<Row>
<Column colMd={8} colLg={8}>

![Image of Video component](../../images/component/video-player/video-player-default.jpg)

</Column>
</Row>

The video can be played in-place of the poster image, or inside the [Lightbox Media Viewer](./lightbox-media-viewer). The later is recommended for a more immersive viewing experience.

![Image of Video component in Lightbox media viewer](../../images/component/lightbox-media-viewer/lightbox-media-viewer-use-case-07.jpg)
