## Overview

The video component defaults to the aspect ratio of the source video, or one of
the standard aspect ratios specified below can be used instead. In most cases
16x9 is recommended.

By default, the component is prefilled with content returned from the Kaltura
service. You can override this default content and provide a custom video title,
description, and poster image. If the content is optional for the component
you're using, you could also remove the prefilled content.

The video can be played in place of the poster image, or inside the
[Lightbox Media Viewer](./lightbox-media-viewer). The latter is recommended for
a more immersive viewing experience.
