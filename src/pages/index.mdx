## The Foundations

Carbon for IBM.com is built on strong foundations. Learn how the IBM Web Standards,
the Carbon Design System, and the IBM Design Language define what's
possible for your page implementations.

<Row>
<Column colMd={2} colLg={4}>

#### IBM Design Language

</Column>
<Column colMd={6} colLg={8}>

The IBM Design Language provides the guidance and assets used to express the IBM brand in products, communications, marketing, events and digital experiences. Carbon for IBM.com looks to the Design Language for the grid best practices, pictogram, and icons.

[IBM Design Language](https://www.ibm.com/design/language/)
