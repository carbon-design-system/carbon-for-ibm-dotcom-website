<PageDescription>

The card section pattern is a collection of cards presented in a full-width section with a left-column header that responds to the grid. When using a call-to-action (CTA) within a card, use an icon. The Primary and Secondary buttons should be reserved for the most important action a user can take on the page.

## Card section &mdash; simple
The card section simple pattern is a collection of text-based cards presented in a full-width section with a left-column header. This pattern is typically used for presenting resources or links.

![Card section simple](../..//images/pattern/card-section/card-section-simple.jpg)

## Card section &mdash; images
The card section images pattern is a collection of card components with images that, together, occupy a full-width section with a left-column header. This pattern is typically used for presenting resources or links.

![Card section images](../..//images/pattern/card-section/card-section-images.jpg)
