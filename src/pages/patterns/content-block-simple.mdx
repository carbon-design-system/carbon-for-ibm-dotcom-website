## Default
Default content block simple pattern is a top-level pattern meaning it ideally lives at the top of a page for introducing page content. It includes a heading, introductory paragraph, optional media (image or video), optional CTA, and horizontal rule.

![Image of content block simple default](../../images/pattern/content-block-simple/default.jpg)

## Content block simple with aside elements
Content block simple with aside elements pattern is also used for introducing page content but includes navigational quick links on the right side. These quick links provide an alternate route directing users to resources related to the content in the main body.

![Image of content block simple with aside elements](../../images/pattern/content-block-simple/with-aside-element.jpg)
