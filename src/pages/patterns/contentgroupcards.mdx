The Content group &mdash; with cards pattern is used to present information through a group of cards with each acting as a call to action that drives to additional or supporting destinations.

This pattern is suitable for adding concise buckets of content and links to a long-form reading experience.

The Content group &mdash; with cards pattern includes a section heading, body copy, and any number of card components listed below. This pattern can be used multiple times within a content block.
