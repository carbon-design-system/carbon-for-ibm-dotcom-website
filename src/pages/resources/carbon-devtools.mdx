## What's included

### Align to grid

Use the grid overlay feature to lay the 2x and mini unit grid on top of your
page to quickly check the alignment of your page's layout.

### Identify tokens

Switch between a number of speccing options like color, spacing, and typography
to identify what Carbon tokens are being used for a component or element on the
page.

### Find components

Get an inventory of all the Carbon supported components being used on a page.
Search for a specific component, and click on it to find where it's located.
